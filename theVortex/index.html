TheVortex

On today's episode of The Vortex, your host, Edgar Tejada, interviews his fellow students, Cathryn Flores, Kristen Islas, and Vishaal Kumar. Each member of the team will be presenting the research and finding of a published author on the scientific literature regarding the past, present, and future of the the Great Pacific Garbage Patch. This rarely discussed pile up of plastic runoff exists between the gyres that span from California to Hawaii.
